ROI Calculator: How to Calculate Return on Investment Easily
Every business wants to know whether its investment is generating profit or not. This is where an ROI calculator becomes useful.
The ROI full form is Return on Investment. It is a simple way to measure how much profit you earn compared to the amount you spend.
Businesses use ROI to track:
Marketing campaigns
Advertising performance
Software investments
Sales growth
Lead generation results

What Is ROI?
Many people ask, what is ROI?
ROI is a financial metric that shows the profitability of an investment. In simple words, it tells you whether your investment was worth the money spent.
Understanding return on investment helps businesses make smarter financial decisions.

How to Calculate ROI
If you are wondering how to calculate ROI, the process is simple.
ROI Calculation Formula
ROI = (Net Profit ÷ Investment Cost) × 100
This basic [ROI calculation](https://leadzen.ai/roi-calculator/) formula helps calculate the percentage return earned from an investment.

ROI Calculation Example
Suppose you spend $1,000 on a marketing campaign and generate $4,000 in revenue.
ROI Calculation:
Revenue = $4,000
Investment Cost = $1,000
Net Profit = $3,000
Formula:
(3000 ÷ 1000) × 100 = 300%
This means your campaign generated a 300% return on investment.
